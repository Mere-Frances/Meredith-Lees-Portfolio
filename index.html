My Story

Kei te rangatira, tēnā koe

My name is Meredith, and I am a creative UI/UX designer, developer and graphic designer. I was born and rasied here in Aotearoa and spent two years of my life persuing my design career in Te Whanganui a Tara, Wellington.

Originally, I spent the first two years of my life after high school at the University of Otago, pursuing a Bachelor of Arts while trying to discover my passions and find my place in the world. It was during these two years that I came to understand my true calling was in the design industry. It wasn't until being encouraged by family and friends to follow my heart and passions that I was able to find the courage to change direction in my career and take a leap of faith.

I started my design career as a black belt beginner and have come a long way in terms of developing my passions, skills, and understanding of the design world, one line of code at a time. I have a natural curiosity for web development that has turned into a personal passion. I especially love the excitement, imagination, and creativity that comes with bringing ideas to life.

  • "Loosies Superette"


    oosie's was another collaborative project, focusing on redesigning the website for the local diner bar, Loosies Superette.

    Together, we created our own assets and graphics while conducting extensive research into the market, and target audience demographic to create a design marketed towards Loosies demographic.

  • "Friends of Golden Bay"

    What started out as an educational assignment, developed into an un-paid charitable project in collaboration with FOGB. The core tools used within this project included extensive brand and user research, wireframing and prototyping, user testing and usability testing, as well as implementing dynamic content, utilizing responsive design, Javascript, SCSS and website deployment.

  • "Maniac Series"


    The Netflix series "Maniac" is a psychological dark comedy, drama that takes place in a modern-day setting but features a Japanese culture, retro-futuristic aesthetic, blending elements from various time periods.
    Developing this website has been one of my all time favorite projects, as it not only involved something I enjoyed, but was a opportunity to experiment creatively.

  • "Amy's Beauty Salon"

    This project was my very first coding assignment, were I put my design skills and knowledge to the test. I was able to create a minimal and functional site design primarily using HTML and css.

    This project sparked my passion for website design and coding, as I thoroughly enjoyed the process. Through receiving encouraging feedback on the design, its functionaliity and especially the fact that this was my first instance producing a website, I was inspired to push myself even further in future projects.

  • "Minecraft Palette Generator"


    This was a collaborative passion project were we experimented with react and Javascript to produce functional palette generator for users to create their own blocks palettes.

  • "The Witchery Hotel"

    This project brief focused around redesigning a current website to meet a new audience, as well as incorporating a responsive design.

    This project was specifically a great challenge for me to practice coding responsive pages as well as experimenting with a new and unfamiliar design aesthetic, which pushed me out of my comfort zone. Ultimately I reached a point were I felt very proud of my process and the product I was able to produce.
